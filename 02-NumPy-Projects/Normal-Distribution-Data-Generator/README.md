# Normal Distribution Data Generator

A Python program that generates samples from a normal distribution and analyzes its statistical properties using NumPy.

## Features

- Generates 1000 random samples from a normal distribution
- Calculates mean of the generated data
- Calculates standard deviation of the generated data
- Demonstrates core concepts in statistics and probability

## How It Works

The program:
1. Generates 1000 random samples from a normal (Gaussian) distribution
2. Uses parameters: mean (loc) = 0, standard deviation (scale) = 1
3. Calculates and displays the mean of the samples
4. Calculates and displays the standard deviation of the samples

## Normal Distribution Explained

The normal distribution (bell curve) is a continuous probability distribution characterized by:
- **Mean (μ)**: The center of the distribution
- **Standard Deviation (σ)**: The spread or width of the distribution

### Properties:
- Symmetric around the mean
- 68% of data falls within ±1σ of the mean
- 95% of data falls within ±2σ of the mean
- 99.7% of data falls within ±3σ of the mean
